Before emailing

What to include and what to avoid.

Helpful details

  • App version and build, if visible in the app.
  • Device model and operating system version.
  • What you were trying to do.
  • What happened instead.
  • Screenshots with private information removed.

Do not send

  • Passwords, private keys, Apple .p8 files, Firebase service account files, or API tokens.
  • Government identity documents or payment information.
  • Screenshots containing private messages, email addresses, or other sensitive personal data unless strictly necessary and redacted.

FAQ

Common support questions.

Can support validate my real PLC program?

No. FactoryLogic PLC is educational software and cannot approve, certify, commission, or validate real industrial control systems.

Can I request account deletion by email?

Yes. Use the account deletion page or email factorylogicplc@gmail.com with the subject FactoryLogic PLC account deletion request.

Should I post private account issues in public GitHub issues?

No. Public issues should not contain personal data, credentials, account information, or screenshots with sensitive data.
