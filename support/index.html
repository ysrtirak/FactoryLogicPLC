FAQ

Common questions.

Can support validate a real PLC program?

No. FactoryLogic PLC is educational software and cannot approve, certify, commission, or validate real industrial control systems.

Can I request account deletion by email?

Yes. Use the account deletion page or email factorylogicplc@gmail.com with the subject FactoryLogic PLC account deletion request.
