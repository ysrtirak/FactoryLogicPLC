What the app does

Learn PLC helps learners build PLC fundamentals through structured chapters and safe simulation exercises. It is designed for education, practice, and concept reinforcement.

Ladder logic practice

Build and inspect ladder logic patterns in guided learning scenarios.

Tags and mappings

Connect simulated components to PLC-style tags and understand signal flow.

Industrial components

Practice with common controls, indicators, motors, sensors, timers, and counters.
