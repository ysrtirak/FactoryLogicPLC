Terms and acceptable use

By using FactoryLogic PLC, you agree to use it as educational software for learning and practice.

Educational use only

FactoryLogic PLC is not certified engineering, commissioning, machine-control, safety-validation, or compliance software.

  • Do not use app results to approve or validate real machinery.
  • Do not use lesson examples as production control logic.
  • Do not treat community answers as professional engineering approval.

Community conduct

Use community features respectfully and lawfully. Do not post personal data, credentials, unsafe instructions, harassment, spam, or content that violates another person's rights.

No warranty

The app is provided for learning and practice. Simulation results, lessons, examples, and community content may be incomplete or incorrect and should not be treated as professional advice.

Changes

Features, learning content, simulation behavior, community availability, and policies may change as the app evolves.
