Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026. This policy explains how FactoryLogic PLC handles information when you use the app, website, learning features, simulation features, account features, and community features.

Privacy contact

For privacy questions, data requests, or account deletion support, contact factorylogicplc@gmail.com.

Information the app may process

Account and profile data

If account features are used, the app may process authentication identifiers, email address, display name, provider information, account status, and optional profile fields such as job title, experience level, location, bio, LinkedIn URL, or contact email.

Learning and simulation data

The app may store learning progress, quiz attempts, scores, unlock state, tags, mappings, ladder drafts, preferences, and simulation state needed to provide the learning experience.

Community content

If community features are used, questions, answers, comments, reports, useful markers, block records, message requests, profile references, moderation state, and hidden/deleted state may be processed.

Diagnostics and service data

The app may process crash reports, device and operating system information, app version, diagnostic events, analytics or reliability events, security logs, and backend operation records.

How information is used

App functionality

Information is used to provide lessons, quizzes, simulation state, ladder logic practice, tags, mappings, account features, saved progress, preferences, and community features.

Support, safety, and security

Information may be used to diagnose bugs, respond to support requests, protect accounts, investigate abuse reports, moderate community content, prevent spam or unsafe content, and comply with legal or app store obligations.

Service providers

FactoryLogic PLC does not sell personal data. Information may be processed by service providers needed to run the app, including Firebase and Google services, app store providers, and infrastructure services.

Public community content

Community posts and profile references may be visible to other users according to the community feature design. Users should not post private or sensitive information.

Retention, deletion, and choices

Retention

Data is retained only as needed for app features, security, moderation, legal obligations, support, reliability, account operations, and service integrity.

Account deletion

Users can request deletion through the account deletion page or by emailing the privacy contact. Authentication, profile information, and account-linked app data are deleted where possible.

Data that may remain

Community posts, comments, answers, reports, moderation records, security logs, fraud-prevention records, audit records, legal records, or dispute-handling records may be anonymized or retained when needed for safety, legal compliance, abuse prevention, discussion integrity, or security.

Children and community features

Community features are age-gated. Users who do not meet the app's age and eligibility requirements should not use community features.

Security

FactoryLogic PLC uses reasonable technical and organizational safeguards. No online system can be guaranteed completely secure. Users should never send passwords, private keys, service account files, API tokens, or identity documents through support channels.

Changes

This policy may be updated when app features, data practices, service providers, or app store requirements change. The updated policy will be published on this website.
