Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026. This policy explains how FactoryLogic PLC handles information when you use the app, website, learning features, simulation features, account features, and community features.

Privacy contact

For privacy questions, data requests, or account deletion support, contact factorylogicplc@gmail.com.

Information we may process

Account and profile information

If account features are used, the app may process authentication identifiers and profile fields needed to provide account and community functionality.

  • User identifier, email address, display name, and authentication provider information.
  • Optional profile information such as job title, experience level, location, bio, LinkedIn URL, or contact email.
  • Account status, eligibility state, and account deletion state.

Learning and simulation data

The app may store progress and app state needed to provide the learning experience.

  • Learning progress, quiz attempts, quiz scores, unlock state, and completion state.
  • Tags, mappings, ladder drafts, chapter progress, preferences, and simulation state.
  • Runtime and troubleshooting information shown inside the app.

Community content

If community features are used, user-generated content and moderation records may be processed.

  • Questions, answers, comments, useful markers, reports, block records, message requests, and profile references.
  • Moderation status, hidden/deleted status, and safety-related review information.
  • Public content may remain visible or be anonymized depending on deletion and moderation needs.

Diagnostics and service data

The app may use Firebase and Google services for authentication, storage, cloud functions, crash reporting, analytics, messaging, and security.

  • Crash reports, app version, operating system, device information, and diagnostic events.
  • Analytics or reliability events used to improve app quality and understand feature usage.
  • Security logs and backend operation records needed to protect the service.

How information is used

App functionality

  • Provide lessons, quizzes, simulation state, ladder logic practice, tags, mappings, and account features.
  • Remember progress, unlocks, preferences, and saved learning state.
  • Provide community posting, answering, reporting, blocking, and moderation features when enabled.

Safety, security, and support

  • Detect crashes, diagnose bugs, protect accounts, investigate abuse reports, and respond to support requests.
  • Moderate community content and protect users from spam, harassment, unsafe content, or policy violations.
  • Comply with legal, security, and app store obligations.

Sharing and service providers

FactoryLogic PLC does not sell personal data. Information may be processed by service providers needed to run the app, including Firebase and Google services, app store providers, and infrastructure services.

  • Data may be shared when necessary to operate app features, comply with law, prevent abuse, or protect users and the service.
  • Public community content is visible to other users according to the community feature design.
  • App distribution, payment, refund, and review systems are handled by the relevant app store providers.

Retention, deletion, and privacy choices

Retention

Data is retained only as needed for app features, security, moderation, legal obligations, support, reliability, and account operations.

Account deletion

Users can request account deletion through the account deletion page or by emailing the privacy contact. When deletion is completed, authentication and account profile data are removed where possible.

  • Learning progress and account-linked app data are deleted where possible.
  • Community posts, answers, comments, reports, and moderation records may be anonymized or retained when needed for safety, moderation, abuse prevention, legal compliance, or discussion integrity.
  • Some records may be retained where required for legal, fraud-prevention, audit, security, or dispute-handling reasons.

Children and community features

Community features are age-gated. Users who do not meet the app's age and eligibility requirements should not use community features.

Security

FactoryLogic PLC uses reasonable technical and organizational safeguards for app data. No online system can be guaranteed completely secure. Users should never send passwords, private keys, service account files, or identity documents through support channels.

Changes

This policy may be updated when app features, data practices, service providers, or app store requirements change. The updated policy will be published on this website.
