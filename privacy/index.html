Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026. This policy explains how Learn PLC handles information when you use the app, including learning, simulation, account, and community features.

Information the app may process

  • Account information provided by authentication providers, such as user identifier, email address, and display name when sign-in is used.
  • Profile information you choose to add, such as job title, experience level, location, bio, LinkedIn URL, or contact email.
  • Community content you create, including questions, answers, comments, reports, and moderation-related records.
  • Learning progress, quiz attempts, completion state, app preferences, and simulation state needed to provide app features.
  • Diagnostics, analytics, crash information, device information, app version, and operating system information used to improve reliability and safety.

How information is used

  • To provide learning paths, simulation state, quizzes, community features, and account functionality.
  • To moderate community content, investigate abuse reports, protect users, and maintain service integrity.
  • To diagnose crashes, measure reliability, understand feature usage, and improve the app.
  • To respond to support, privacy, and account deletion requests.

Service providers

Learn PLC may use Firebase and other Google services for authentication, storage, analytics, crash reporting, cloud functions, messaging, and security. App distribution and payments, if any, are handled by the relevant app store provider.

Data sharing

Learn PLC does not sell personal data. Information may be shared with service providers that operate app features, comply with legal obligations, prevent abuse, or protect the rights and safety of users and the service.

Retention and deletion

Data is retained only as needed for app features, security, moderation, legal obligations, and support. When an account deletion request is completed, authentication and account records are removed where possible. Community content may be anonymized instead of hard-deleted when needed to preserve discussion integrity, moderation records, safety, or abuse-prevention history.

Privacy choices

You can request account deletion and associated data deletion through the account deletion page. You can also use the support contact published with the app store listing for privacy questions.

Children and community features

Community features are intended only for users who meet the age and eligibility requirements shown in the app. Users who do not meet those requirements should not use community features.

Security

Administrative credentials, private keys, service account files, and signing keys must never be uploaded through support channels or public repositories.

Changes

This policy may be updated when app features, data practices, or store requirements change. The updated policy will be published on this website.

Learn PLC is an educational simulator and is not certified for commissioning, industrial safety validation, or real-world machine control.
