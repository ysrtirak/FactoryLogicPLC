Privacy Policy

Privacy policy for FactoryLogic PLC.

Last updated: May 20, 2026. How FactoryLogic PLC handles information for the app, website, account, community, and support.

Privacy contact

For privacy questions, data requests, or account deletion support, contact factorylogicplc@gmail.com.

Information we may process

Account and profile

Authentication identifiers, email address, display name, provider information, account status, and optional profile details.

Learning and simulation

Learning progress, quiz attempts, scores, unlock state, tags, mappings, ladder drafts, preferences, and simulation state.

Community content

Questions, answers, comments, reports, moderation state, message requests, block records, and profile references.

Diagnostics and service data

App version, device and operating system information, crash reports, diagnostics, analytics or reliability events, security logs, and backend operation records.

How information is used

App functionality

To provide lessons, saved progress, quizzes, simulation state, tags, mappings, account features, and community features.

Support and security

To diagnose bugs, answer support requests, protect accounts, moderate community content, prevent abuse, and meet legal or app store obligations.

Service providers

FactoryLogic PLC does not sell personal data. Data may be processed by services needed to run the app, including Firebase and Google services, app store providers, and infrastructure services.

Retention and deletion

Retention

Data is retained only as needed for app features, security, moderation, legal obligations, support, reliability, account operations, and service integrity.

Account deletion

Users can request deletion through the account deletion page or by emailing the privacy contact. Authentication, profile information, and account-linked app data are deleted where possible.

Records that may remain

Community content, moderation records, security logs, fraud-prevention records, audit records, legal records, or dispute-handling records may be anonymized or retained when needed for safety, legal compliance, abuse prevention, discussion integrity, or security.

Children and community features

Community features are age-gated. Users who do not meet the app's age and eligibility requirements should not use community features.

Security and changes

Reasonable safeguards are used, but no online system can be guaranteed completely secure. This policy may be updated when features, service providers, or app store requirements change.

Privacy contact.

For privacy inquiries, data requests, or account deletion support, email FactoryLogic PLC.
