Account Deletion

How to request deletion of a FactoryLogic PLC account and associated data.

Account deletion

Request account and data deletion.

If you created or used an account in FactoryLogic PLC, you can request deletion of your account and associated data outside the app from this page.

Send a deletion request

Email FactoryLogic PLC with the subject line below. Send the request from the email address connected to your account when possible.

Email deletion request Read privacy policy

What to include

  • The email address connected to the account, if available.
  • Display name or profile details that help identify the account.
  • A short statement that you want your FactoryLogic PLC account deleted.

Do not include

  • Passwords, private keys, service account files, or authentication tokens.
  • Government identity documents unless support specifically asks for a lawful verification step.
  • Public GitHub issue links for private account deletion requests.

Process

What happens after a request.

Deletion requests are reviewed so the correct account is identified and protected from unauthorized deletion.

  1. 01Request received

    Support receives your deletion request at factorylogicplc@gmail.com.

  2. 02Account matched

    The account is matched using the email address and account details you provide.

  3. 03Deletion processed

    Authentication, account profile, and account-linked app data are deleted where possible.

  4. 04Community data handled

    Community content may be anonymized or retained where needed for safety, moderation, legal compliance, abuse prevention, or discussion integrity.

Deletion scope

Data normally deleted

  • Authentication account where deletion is possible.
  • Account profile information.
  • Learning progress and account-linked app records where possible.
  • Local account state after the app completes deletion or sign-out.

Data that may be anonymized or retained

  • Community posts, answers, comments, reports, and moderation records when needed for thread integrity, user safety, abuse prevention, legal compliance, security, or audit integrity.
  • Security, fraud-prevention, audit, legal, or dispute-handling records for the period required by law or legitimate safety needs.
  • Data controlled by app store providers or third-party identity providers according to their own policies.
