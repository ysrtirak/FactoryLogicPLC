Account deletion

Request account deletion.

If you created or used a FactoryLogic PLC account, you can request deletion from this page without signing in.

Send a deletion request

Email FactoryLogic PLC with the subject below. Send the request from the email address connected to your account when possible.

Email deletion request Privacy policy

Include

  • The email address connected to the account, if available.
  • Display name or profile details that help identify the account.
  • A short statement that you want your FactoryLogic PLC account deleted.

Do not include

  • Passwords, private keys, service account files, authentication tokens, payment details, or identity documents.
  • Private account deletion requests in public GitHub issues.

What happens next.

  1. 01Request received

    Support receives your email at factorylogicplc@gmail.com.

  2. 02Account matched

    The account is matched using the email address and details you provide.

  3. 03Deletion handled

    Account data is deleted where possible. Some records may be anonymized or retained when needed for safety, moderation, legal compliance, abuse prevention, or security.

Deletion scope

Normally deleted

  • Authentication account where deletion is possible.
  • Account profile information.
  • Learning progress and account-linked app records where possible.

May be retained or anonymized

  • Community posts, answers, comments, reports, and moderation records when needed for thread integrity, user safety, abuse prevention, legal compliance, security, or audit integrity.
  • Security, fraud-prevention, audit, legal, or dispute-handling records for the period required by law or legitimate safety needs.
  • Data controlled by app store providers or third-party identity providers according to their own policies.
