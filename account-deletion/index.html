Account deletion

If you created or used an account in Learn PLC, you can request deletion of your account and associated data.

How to request deletion

  • Use the private support contact published with the app store listing and request Learn PLC account deletion.
  • Send the request from the email address connected to the account when possible.
  • If you cannot access that email address, include enough non-sensitive information to help identify the account.
  • Do not post deletion requests, passwords, private keys, authentication tokens, or identity documents in public GitHub issues.

What is deleted or anonymized

  • Authentication records and account profile data are deleted where possible.
  • Learning progress and account-linked app data are deleted where possible.
  • Community posts, answers, comments, and reports may be anonymized instead of hard-deleted when needed for safety, moderation, abuse prevention, or discussion integrity.
  • Some records may be retained when required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, audit integrity, or dispute handling.

After deletion

Deleted accounts may lose access to learning progress, community activity, messages, saved settings, and any account-linked features.

Learn PLC is an educational simulator and is not certified for commissioning, industrial safety validation, or real-world machine control.
